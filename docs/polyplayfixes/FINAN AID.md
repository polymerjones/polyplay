Which WASFA application year should I fill out?

- Complete the 2025-26 WASFA application if you are enrolling in college (as a new student or continuing student) in fall term 2025 through spring term 2026.
- Complete the 2026-27 WASFA application if you will enroll in college in fall term 2026 through spring term 2027.
- Planning on taking courses in the summer? Please check with the college you will be attending to confirm which application you need to complete if you are enrolling in summer sessions.
