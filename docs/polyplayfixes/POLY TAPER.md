
A simple one screen app that tracks medication usage.

It's basically an accountability app that makes tracking medication intake FAST, and easy and visually appealing.

The idea is, user can quickly open the app, press the button, and go on with their day.

At the top there is a box that has a motivational quote with a button than flashes and goes to the next quote (basically the code I have for my PolyPlay bible verse box)

You decide the medication you're going to taper (only one at a time). You enter how many pills are remaining.

App has a big visually appealing x`central button that user presses when user takes their medication.

The date and time is saved. The pills remaining counter at the bottom subtracts one.

Pressing the button opens up a modal to confirm you're taking the medication (button is not spammable)

- OPTIONAL: A time schedule can be set so the button is greyed out until the time to take it.

 - A countdown that shows time remaining before next dose.
